This page describes opportunities and impacts arising from the rapidly evolving new technology of generative AI

Generative AI is when artificial intelligence that produces models are used to create products through imitating the underlying patterns and structures of trained data.

Generative AI by finding source material and adapting it through machine learning is able to produce text, images, videos, and audio.


Real-world applications

This technology is used in programming, healthcare, finance, entertainment, service business, writing, and product design.

This is because of its diverse knowledge and uses gained from absorbing massive amounts of content.

Impacts of the technology

The technology is able to adopt and produce effective design quickly, but has a major environmental impact and many are concerned about their data being used in its training.
